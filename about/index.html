Introduction of Our Research

Molecular dynamics(MD) simulation is a kind of essential method that can be utilized to reveal the mechanism of experiment results, predict the results and design the complicated systems so it is used in many different subjects such as chemistry, biology and material science. The MD simulation is also a key method in computional chemistry, bioinformatics and computional biology. In particular, our lab mainly focuses on the molecular dynamics research of biological systems, especially development and application of force field parameters, supramolecular recognition and assembly and protein structure and function. We have done many works in metal ions force field parameterization and metalloprotein MD simulations, protein crowded effect and so on. And we are also interested in enzyme mechanism, liquid-liquid seperation and molecular docking.
