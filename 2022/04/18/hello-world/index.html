Welcome to Zhanglab!

ZhangLab@USTB 北京科技大学张海洋实验室
2022-04-18

Our lab focuses on the molecular dynamics simulation for biological systems, especially development and application of force field parameters and protein structure and function.

We have done many works in metal ions force field parameterization and metalloprotein MD simulations, protein crowded effect and so on. And we are also interested in enzyme mechanism, liquid-liquid seperation, molecular docking, etc.
