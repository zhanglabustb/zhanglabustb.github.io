 Welcome to ZhangLab! We are at School of Chemistry and Biological Engineering, University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB) , China🇨🇳. ZhangLab is the laboratory of Computational Molecular Biology, including Bioinformatics for omics data and Computational Biology related to computational chemistry. We mainly focus the applications of molecular simulation for metal ions and biomacromolecule systems (especially molecular dynamics simulation and molecular docking), computer aided drug design and omics big data mining and analysis.

  欢迎来到张海洋实验室！我们在北京科技大学化学与生物工程学院。我们的研究方向是计算分子生物学，包括了组学数据处理的生物信息学和与计算化学紧密相关的计算生物学。目前我们主要关注分子模拟在金属离子及生物大分子体系中的应用(特别是分子动力学模拟和分子对接)、计算机辅助药物设计和组学大数据挖掘和分析。
