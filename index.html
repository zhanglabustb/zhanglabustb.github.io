Welcome to ZhangLab! We are at School of Chemistry and Biological Engineering, University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB). ZhangLab is the laboratory of Computational Molecular Biology, including Bioinformatics for omics data and Computational Biology related to computational chemistry. We mainly focus the applications of molecular dynamics simulation for biomacromolecule systems, computer aided drug design and omics big data mining and analysis. 欢迎来到张海洋实验室！我们在北京科技大学化学与生物工程学院。我们的研究方向是 计算分子生物学，包括组学数据处理的生物信息学和与计算化学紧密相关的计算生物学。目前我们主要关注分子动力学模拟在生物大分子体系中的应用、计算机辅助药物设计和组学大数据挖掘和分析。
